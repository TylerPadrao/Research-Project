The change and upgrading of technology due to COVID-19 remote worker

Electronic Supply Chain

As many workers scramble to work from home, the hardware companies have seen a major demand for laptops and mobile devices to support employees working at home. The supply of electronic devices has become scarce. Factories in Asia are struggling to open at full capacity and the shortage of raw materials has caused a disruption to the electronics value chain which has decreased the availability of products and continues to increase product prices (Deloitte, 2020).

Security

Security software will see a benefit from a growing remote workforce. Companies now have to manage and organize everything remotely across many different networks and ascertain a secure infrastructure for remote working. As cybercrime increased since the pandemic, in depth security structural changes are required to protect the work remotely (Belgium, 2020).

Technical Communication Tools

Technology has played a huge role in communication remotely. Companies think they have the tools to work remotely. However, there is a lack of communication tools and training of how employees should navigate remote work situations. The increase use of teleconferencing software such as instant messaging tools, project management tools, time tracking tools, and video conferencing are all good technologies for a remote worker to take advantage. These tools will all assist with a productive work environment (Shukla, 2020).

Remote Worker & Technology

An average US employee that works from home spends 5 hours a week dealing with technology problems. This advised employers to strengthen their technology. Employers need to implement remote working in a structural way; without the correct tools and structure to implement remote working, there is a risk of loss of efficiency and frustration with employees and stakeholders (Loten, 2020).
