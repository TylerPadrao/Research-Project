Overview

COVID has impacted the entire world. Almost 70% of Americans during the height of COVID were working from home. Although remote working has been on the rise before the pandemic, it has increased substantially. Even when stay at home orders are relaxed, many workers may continue to work remotely.

How does working from home impact the worker? Evidence has shown both positive and negative effects on emotional and physical wellbeing. Things to consider when working from home are work life balance, productivity, flexibility, and collaboration. Additionally, COVID has changed the IT strategy and has transformed businesses. Executives recognize advanced technologies as a critical component of a business. COVID has impacted the electronic supply chain, security, and remote communication technologies.
